# Question 9

The answer of the operation (10111) 2 * (1110) 2 in hex equivalence is

- **A.** 150
- **B.** 241
- **C.** 142
- **D.** 101011110

## Answer

**C. 142**

## Detailed solution

(10111)₂=16+4+2+1=23 and (1110)₂=14. Their product is 23×14=322. Repeated division or place values give 322=1×16²+4×16+2=(142)₁₆.

## Why the other options do not work

150₁₆ and 241₁₆ represent different decimal values, while option D is a binary-looking string rather than the requested hexadecimal representation.

## Key idea to remember

Convert, multiply, and regroup the product into hexadecimal place values.
