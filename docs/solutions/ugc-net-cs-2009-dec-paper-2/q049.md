# Question 49

Another name of IEEE 802.11 a is ____________

- **A.** Wi-Max
- **B.** Fast Ethernet
- **C.** Wi-Fi
- **D.** 802.11 g

## Answer

**C. Wi-Fi**

## Detailed solution

IEEE 802.11a is one member of the IEEE 802.11 wireless LAN family marketed under the Wi-Fi interoperability name. It operates in the 5 GHz range and is therefore Wi-Fi.

## Why the other options do not work

WiMAX is based on IEEE 802.16, Fast Ethernet is IEEE 802.3 networking, and 802.11g is a different Wi-Fi amendment rather than another name for 802.11a.

## Key idea to remember

802.11 denotes Wi-Fi WLAN standards; 802.16 denotes WiMAX.
