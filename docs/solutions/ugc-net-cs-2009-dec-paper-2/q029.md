# Question 29

In case of Bus/Tree topology signal balancing issue is overcome by

- **A.** Modulation
- **B.** Polling
- **C.** Segmentation
- **D.** Strong transmitter

## Answer

**C. Segmentation**

## Detailed solution

A large bus/tree cable can suffer unequal attenuation and signal levels over distance. Dividing it into shorter segments connected by repeaters limits each electrical span and regenerates the signal, overcoming the balancing problem. This is segmentation.

## Why the other options do not work

Polling controls access, modulation changes signal representation, and merely using a stronger transmitter does not correctly regenerate and isolate long cable sections.

## Key idea to remember

Segment long shared media so repeaters can restore signal quality between spans.
