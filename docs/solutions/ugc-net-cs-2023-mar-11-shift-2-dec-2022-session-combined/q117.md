# Question 117

harpin 10-35031 10:187067 Match List I with List II LIST II LIST I A. Direct Addressing MVIA, FFH Indirect Addressing II. MOVA, B III. STA FFFFH Register Addressing D. Immediate Addressing IV. MOVA. M Choose the correct answer from the options given below:

- **1.** A-I, B-II, C-III, D-IV
- **2.** A-II, B-I, C-IV, D-I
- **3.** A-I, B-IV, C-I, D-I
- **4.** A-IV, B-III, C-I, D-II

## Answer

**3. A-I, B-IV, C-I, D-I**

## Detailed solution

STA FFFFH uses direct addressing (A–III); MOV A,M obtains memory through HL, so indirect (B–IV); MOV A,B is register addressing (C–II); and MVI A,FFH embeds immediate data (D–I).

## Why the other options do not work

The other options mismatch at least one mnemonic with its operand source.

## Key idea to remember

Immediate embeds data; register names a register; direct embeds an address; indirect obtains it from a register pair.
