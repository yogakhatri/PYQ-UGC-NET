# Question 80

l. No.3 BID:87031 Which of the following is &-free grammar?

- **1.** Type-0
- **2.** Type-1
- **3.** Type-2
- **4.** Тype-3

## Answer

**2. Type-1**

## Detailed solution

A context-sensitive (Type-1) grammar uses noncontracting productions, so it is ε-free except for the conventional optional S→ε case when S never appears on a right-hand side.

## Why the other options do not work

Unrestricted and context-free grammars may contain ε-productions; regular grammars may also represent ε under standard conventions.

## Key idea to remember

Type-1 productions do not shorten strings, hence no ordinary ε-productions.
