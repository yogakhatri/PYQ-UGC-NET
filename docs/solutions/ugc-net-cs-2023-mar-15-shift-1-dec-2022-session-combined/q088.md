# Question 88

S10:37038 What is the process of capturing inference process as a single inference rule?

- **1.** Ponens
- **2.** Clauses
- **3.** Generalized modus ponens
- **4.** Variables

## Answer

**3. Generalized modus ponens**

## Detailed solution

Generalized modus ponens packages matching/unification and implication application into one inference rule for first-order Horn-style knowledge bases.

## Why the other options do not work

‘Ponens’ alone names the propositional rule; clauses and variables are representations, not the requested inference-capturing process.

## Key idea to remember

Generalized modus ponens extends modus ponens with substitutions/unification.
