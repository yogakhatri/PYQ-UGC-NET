# Question 104

10:87054 Given below are two statements: Statement I: Software testing must be recognized as a significant effort, and its planning must begin at the earliest possible time. Statement Il: The cost of software testing is an important contribution in determining overall software development costs. In the light of the above statements, choose the correct answer from the options given below

- **1.** Both Statement I and Statement Il are true.
- **2.** Both Statement I and Statement Il are false.
- **3.** Statement I is true but Statement Il is false.
- **4.** Statement I is false but Statement Il is true.

## Answer

**1. Both Statement I and Statement Il are true.**

## Detailed solution

Testing is a substantial lifecycle activity and should be planned from the beginning. Its labor, environments, automation, and defect-repair work contribute materially to total development cost. Both statements are true.

## Why the other options do not work

Options 2–4 incorrectly deny early planning or testing's cost impact.

## Key idea to remember

Test planning begins early even though execution spans later stages.
