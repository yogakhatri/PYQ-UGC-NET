# Question 125

Match List I with List I! List II List I I. Unsupervised Learning. A. Regression B. KNN Il. Supervised Learning. C. Clustering D. Decision Tree Choose the correct answer from the options given below:

- **1.** A-I, B-II. C-I , D-I
- **2.** A-II , B-II, C-1, D- Il
- **3.** A-1, B- II, C-"I , D-1
- **4.** A-II , B-II , C-II, D-I

## Answer

**2. A-II , B-II, C-1, D- Il**

## Detailed solution

Regression, KNN, and decision trees learn from labeled targets and are supervised (A,B,D→II). Clustering discovers unlabeled groups and is unsupervised (C→I).

## Why the other options do not work

Other mappings classify regression/decision trees as unsupervised or clustering as supervised.

## Key idea to remember

Labels distinguish supervised prediction from unsupervised structure discovery.
