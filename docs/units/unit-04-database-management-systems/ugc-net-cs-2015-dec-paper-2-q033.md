# Question 33

*UGC NET CS · 2015 Dec Paper 2 · Data Warehousing and Data Mining · OLAP and OLTP*

__________ system is market oriented and is used for data analysis by knowledge workers including Managers, Executives and Analysts.

- **1.** OLTP
- **2.** OLAP
- **3.** Data System
- **4.** Market System

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 2. OLAP**

## Solution

OLAP—Online Analytical Processing—is designed for multidimensional, historical, market-oriented analysis by managers, executives, and analysts. It supports complex queries, aggregation, drill-down, roll-up, and comparison for decision-making. Therefore OLAP fits the description.

## Key Points

- OLTP runs operations; OLAP analyzes accumulated data for decisions.

## Why the other options are incorrect

OLTP supports frequent day-to-day transactions with short update-oriented operations. ‘Data System’ and ‘Market System’ are not the standard processing categories contrasted in data warehousing.
