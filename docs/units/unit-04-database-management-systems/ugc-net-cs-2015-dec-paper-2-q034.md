# Question 34

*UGC NET CS · 2015 Dec Paper 2 · Data Warehousing and Data Mining · Data Warehouse Views*

__________ allows selection of the relevant information necessary for the data warehouse.

- **1.** The Top - Down View
- **2.** Data Warehouse View
- **3.** Datasource View
- **4.** Business Query View

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 1. The Top - Down View**

## Solution

In the standard four-view description of a data warehouse, the top-down view selects the relevant information needed for the warehouse. It establishes the overall business scope before the physical warehouse schema or user queries are considered. Hence option 1 is correct.

## Key Points

- Top-down view chooses what information belongs in the warehouse.

## Why the other options are incorrect

The data-source view describes operational source information; the data-warehouse view describes warehouse facts and dimensions; the business-query view presents data from an end user's analytical perspective. These views serve later or different purposes.
