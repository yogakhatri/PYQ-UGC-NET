# Question 47

*UGC NET CS · 2009 Dec Paper 2 · Database System Concepts and Architecture · Database Languages and Interfaces*

Analysis of large database to retrieve information is ca lled

- **A.** OLTP
- **B.** OLAP
- **C.** OLDP
- **D.** OLPP

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: B. OLAP**

## Solution

Online Analytical Processing (OLAP) supports multidimensional, aggregate, and exploratory queries over large databases/data warehouses to retrieve decision-support information.

## Key Points

- OLTP runs operations; OLAP analyzes accumulated data.

## Why the other options are incorrect

OLTP is optimized for numerous short operational transactions and updates. OLDP and OLPP are not the standard term for large-scale analytical querying.
