# Question 47

*UGC NET CS · 2016 July Paper 2 · Data Warehousing and Data Mining · Business Intelligence Applications*

Consider the following two statements : (A) Business intelligence and Data warehousing is used for forecasting and Data mining. (B) Business intelligence and Data warehousing is used for analysis of large volumes of sales data. Which one of the following options is correct ?

- **1.** (A) is true, (B) is false.
- **2.** Both (A) and (B) are true.
- **3.** (A) is false, (B) is true.
- **4.** Both (A) and (B) are false.

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 2. Both (A) and (B) are true.**

## Solution

Statement A is true: a data warehouse integrates historical data, while business-intelligence and data-mining tools use it for forecasting and pattern discovery. Statement B is also true: sales analysis is a standard warehouse workload because large transaction histories can be summarized by product, customer, region, and time. Hence both statements are true.

## Key Points

- A data warehouse supports large-scale historical analysis; BI/data mining use it for reporting, forecasting, and pattern discovery.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Options 1 and 3 incorrectly reject one valid use, and option 4 rejects both. Data warehousing supplies consistent, analysis-ready history; BI and mining turn that history into reports, trends, forecasts, and discovered patterns.
