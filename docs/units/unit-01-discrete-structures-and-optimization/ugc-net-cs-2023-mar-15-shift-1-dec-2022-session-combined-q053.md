# Question 53

*UGC NET CS · 2023 Mar 15 Shift 1 Dec 2022 Session · Counting, Mathematical Induction and Discrete Probability · Multinomial coefficients*

The coefficients of x?y3z? in the expansion of (x+y+z) is

- **1.** C(7,3) * C(5,2)
- **2.** C(7,2) * C(5,3)
- **3.** C(7,3) * C(7,2)
- **4.** C(5,3) * C(5,2)

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 2. C(7,2) * C(5,3)**

## Solution

The multinomial coefficient of x²y³z² in (x+y+z)^7 is 7!/(2!3!2!). Choose the two x positions and then three y positions: C(7,2)C(5,3)=210.

## Key Points

- Coefficient of x^a y^b z^c is (a+b+c)!/(a!b!c!).

## Why the other options are incorrect

Other products choose the wrong number of remaining positions and do not equal the multinomial coefficient.
