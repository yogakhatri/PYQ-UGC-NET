# Question 115

*UGC NET CS · 2023 Mar 15 Shift 1 Dec 2022 Session · Knowledge Representation · Limitations of rule-based learning*

510-6565 Consider the following draw backs is/are of Rule based approach is/are: A. The learning is static. B. Any changes made to the original data will not be considered C. Once trained the model cannot improve on the basis of feedback. Choose the correct answer from the options given below:

- **1.** All the above statements are correct
- **2.** The statements A & B only are not correct
- **3.** The statements B & Conly are not correct
- **4.** All the above statements are not correct

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 1. All the above statements are correct**

## Solution

A fixed rule base learns statically, does not automatically incorporate later data changes, and does not improve from feedback after construction unless rules are explicitly revised. A,B,C are all drawbacks.

## Key Points

- Rule systems execute explicit knowledge; adaptive learning requires a separate update mechanism.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Options 2–4 deny one or more characteristic limitations of hand-authored rule systems.
