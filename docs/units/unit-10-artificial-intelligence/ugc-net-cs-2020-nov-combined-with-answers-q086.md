# Question 86

*UGC NET CS · 2020 Nov With Answers · Artificial Intelligence · AI Problem-Solving Characteristics*

Which of the following is NOT true in problem solving in artificial intelligence?

- **1.** Implements heuristic search techniques
- **2.** Solution steps are not explicit
- **3.** Knowledge is imprecise
- **4.** It works on or implements repetition mechanism

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 4. It works on or implements repetition mechanism**

## Solution

AI problem solving is commonly characterized by search through a state space, frequent use of heuristics, and reasoning with incomplete or imprecise knowledge. The route to a solution may emerge through search rather than being listed in advance as a fixed sequence of explicit steps. A generic ‘repetition mechanism,’ however, is an ordinary control feature of conventional algorithms and is not a distinguishing characteristic of AI problem solving. Hence statement 4 is the one that is NOT true in this classification.

## Key Points

- Separate AI search and knowledge characteristics from generic programming control structures such as repetition.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Heuristic search is central to many AI solvers. Non-explicit solution paths describe search procedures that discover a path at run time, and imprecise knowledge is routinely handled by AI representations and inference methods.
