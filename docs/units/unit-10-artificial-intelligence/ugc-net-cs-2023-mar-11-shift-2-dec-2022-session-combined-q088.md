# Question 88

*UGC NET CS · 2023 Mar 11 Shift 2 Dec 2022 Session · Artificial Neural Networks · Unsupervised and Reinforcement Learning*

3 Option 10=394471 Which Artificial intelligence technique enables the computers to understand the associations and relationships between objects & Events?

- **1.** Heuristic Processing.
- **2.** Cognitive Science.
- **3.** Relative symbolism.
- **4.** Pattern Matching.

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 3. Relative symbolism.**

## Solution

Relational symbolism represents objects and events symbolically together with the relations that connect them. This lets a program encode and reason about associations such as is-a, part-of, causes, or precedes.

## Key Points

- Symbolic AI models knowledge using entities plus explicit relations among them.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Heuristic processing guides search, cognitive science is the broader interdisciplinary field, and pattern matching compares structures but is not the general representation technique described.
