# Question 140

*UGC NET CS · 2023 Mar 15 Shift 1 Dec 2022 Session · Artificial Neural Networks · Unsupervised and Reinforcement Learning*

BID:87090 Given below are two statements: one is labelled as Assertion A and the other is labelled as Reason R. A: Artificial Neural Networks are the backbone of deep learning algorithms. R. Back propagation is one of the techniques used to train an ANN. In the light of the above statements, choose the most appropriate answer from the options given below:

- **1.** Both A and Rare correct and Ris the correct explanation of A.
- **2.** Both A and Rare correct but Ris NOT the correct explanation of A.
- **3.** A is correct but R is not correct.
- **4.** A is not correct but R is correct.

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 2. Both A and Rare correct but Ris NOT the correct explanation of A.**

## Solution

Artificial neural networks form the model family used by deep-learning systems, so Assertion A is true. Backpropagation is indeed a standard method for computing gradients used to train neural networks, so Reason R is also true. However, naming one training technique does not explain why ANNs constitute the backbone/model structure of deep learning. Thus R is not the correct explanation of A.

## Key Points

- Two statements may both be true without the reason logically explaining the assertion.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Option 1 confuses a related fact with a causal explanation, while options 3 and 4 incorrectly mark one of the two true statements false.
