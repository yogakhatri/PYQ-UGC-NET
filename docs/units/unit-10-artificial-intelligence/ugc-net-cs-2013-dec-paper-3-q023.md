# Question 23

*UGC NET CS · 2013 Dec Paper 3 · Natural-Language Processing · Pragmatic Knowledge*

High level knowledge which relates to the use of sentences in different contexts and how the context affect the meaning of the sentences ?

- **A.** Morphological
- **B.** Syntactic
- **C.** Semantic
- **D.** Pragmatic

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: D. Pragmatic**

## Solution

Pragmatics studies how context, speaker intention and situation affect the interpretation of an utterance. The same sentence can convey different meanings in different conversational settings, which is exactly the knowledge described.

## Key Points

- NLP levels: morphology-word form, syntax-structure, semantics-literal meaning, pragmatics-contextual meaning.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Morphology studies word structure, syntax studies grammatical arrangement, and semantics studies conventional literal meaning. Context-dependent intended meaning is pragmatic.
