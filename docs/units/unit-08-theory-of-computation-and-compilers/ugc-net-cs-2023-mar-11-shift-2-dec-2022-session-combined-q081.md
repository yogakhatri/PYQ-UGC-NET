# Question 81

*UGC NET CS · 2023 Mar 11 Shift 2 Dec 2022 Session · Syntax Analysis · LR items*

D: 387031 The item generated by the production A→s is :

- **1.** A→.
- **2.** A→ 8.
- **3.** A→.&
- **4.** A→E

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 1. A→.**

## Solution

An LR item marks how much of a production’s right-hand side has been recognized. For an ε-production A→ε, the right-hand side has length zero, so there is no grammar symbol for the dot to cross. Its completed item is represented as A→· (often written [A→·]).

## Key Points

- An ε-production has an empty right-hand side, so its LR item is complete immediately.

## Why the other options are incorrect

ε denotes the empty string, not a terminal that is shifted by the parser. Forms placing a dot before or after a literal ε incorrectly treat ε as an input symbol, while the production without a dot is not an LR item.
