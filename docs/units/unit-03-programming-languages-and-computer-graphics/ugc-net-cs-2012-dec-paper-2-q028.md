# Question 28

*UGC NET CS · 2012 Dec Paper 2 · Web Programming · Electronic Payment Security*

Identify the risk factors associated with an electronic-payment system:

- **1.** Fraudulent use of credit cards
- **2.** Sending credit-card details over the Internet
- **3.** Remote storage of credit-card details
- **4.** All of the above

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 4. All of the above**

## Solution

All three activities create exposure in an electronic-payment system. Stolen or fabricated card credentials can be used fraudulently; card data can be intercepted or redirected while transmitted; and remotely stored credentials can be breached later. Secure systems reduce these risks with authenticated payment flows, encrypted transport, tokenization, restricted storage and monitoring.

## Key Points

- Payment-card risk exists across the credential lifecycle: use, transmission and storage all need protection.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Options 1, 2 and 3 each identify a real risk but each alone is incomplete. The question asks for the risk factors collectively, so 'all of the above' is the complete choice.
