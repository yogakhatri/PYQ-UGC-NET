# Question 50

*UGC NET CS · 2015 Dec Paper 2 · Web Programming · Electronic Banking and Funds Transfer*

__________ uses electronic means to transfer funds directly from one account to another rather than by cheque or cash.

- **1.** M-Banking
- **2.** E-Banking
- **3.** O-Banking
- **4.** C-Banking

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 2. E-Banking**

## Solution

Electronic banking (E-Banking) uses electronic channels to perform banking services, including transferring funds directly between accounts instead of using cash or cheques. This is the general category described by the stem.

## Key Points

- E-Banking is the umbrella term for electronically delivered banking and funds transfer.

## Why the other options are incorrect

M-Banking is specifically banking through mobile devices and is a subset of electronic banking. O-Banking and C-Banking are not the standard general term intended by this definition.
