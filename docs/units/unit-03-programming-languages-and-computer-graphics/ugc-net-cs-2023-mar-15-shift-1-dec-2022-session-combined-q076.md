# Question 76

*UGC NET CS · 2023 Mar 15 Shift 1 Dec 2022 Session · Computer Graphics · Frequency-domain multimedia compression*

0:87026 _ techniques used to encode digital video and audio information and helps analyse the signal in FFTs are domain.

- **1.** Encryption, time
- **2.** Encryption, frequency
- **3.** Compression, time
- **4.** Compression, frequency

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 4. Compression, frequency**

## Solution

Compression techniques encode digital audio/video efficiently. FFTs decompose signals into frequency components, so compression analysis such as transform coding operates in the frequency domain.

## Key Points

- FFT converts time-domain samples into a frequency-domain representation useful for transform compression.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Encryption protects confidentiality rather than reducing representation size; time-domain is inconsistent with the stated FFT clue.
