# Question 44

*UGC NET CS · 2012 June Paper 2 · Web Programming · E-Commerce Applications*

Which is not an e-commerce application?

- **1.** Home banking
- **2.** Buying stocks
- **3.** Conducting an auction
- **4.** Evaluating an employee

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 4. Evaluating an employee**

## Solution

Home banking, online stock trading and online auctions all conduct commercial or financial transactions over electronic networks, so they are standard e-commerce applications. Evaluating an employee is an internal human-resource management activity and is not inherently an e-commerce transaction.

## Key Points

- E-commerce concerns electronically mediated market or financial transactions, not every activity performed with software.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Options 1-3 exchange money, financial instruments or goods/services electronically. They fit the scope of e-commerce, unlike the HR appraisal task.
