*UGC NET CS · 2023 Mar 15 Shift 1 Dec 2022 Session · Microprogrammed Control · Design of Control Unit*

il. No.6' BID:8706 Match List I with List II List | List II A. DAA I. Program Control instruction B. LX| II. Interrupt instruction C. RST III. Data Movement Instruction D. JMP IV. Athematic Instruction Choose the correct answer from the options given below:

- **1.** A-IV, B-II, C-I, D-III
- **2.** A-IV, B-III, C-II, D-I
- **3.** A-IV, B-II, C-III, D-I
- **4.** A-III, B-IV, C-lI, D-l

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 2. A-IV, B-III, C-II, D-I**

## Solution

8085 DAA is arithmetic (A–IV), LXI moves immediate 16-bit data (B–III), RST is a restart/interrupt instruction (C–II), and JMP controls program flow (D–I).

## Key Points

- Classify instructions by their primary architectural effect.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Other options misclassify data movement, interrupts, or jumps.
