# Question 46

*UGC NET CS · 2017 Nov Paper 2 · Parallel Computer Architecture · Historical TOP500 Supercomputer Rankings*

Which of the following Super Computers is the fastest Super Computer ?

- **1.** Sunway TaihuLight
- **2.** Titan
- **3.** Piz Daint
- **4.** Sequoia

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 1. Sunway TaihuLight**

## Solution

This is a time-dependent 2017 question. On the November 2017 TOP500 list, Sunway TaihuLight ranked first with a measured HPL performance of about 93.0 petaflops. Piz Daint ranked third, while Titan and Sequoia were lower. Therefore option 1 was correct at the time of the examination.

## Key Points

- For historical technology questions, anchor the fact to the exam date and the benchmark/list being referenced—in this case TOP500 HPL, November 2017.

## Why the other options are incorrect

The other three were major supercomputers, but none outranked Sunway TaihuLight on the relevant 2017 TOP500 list. The answer must not be interpreted as a present-day ranking because the world's fastest system changes as new machines enter service.
