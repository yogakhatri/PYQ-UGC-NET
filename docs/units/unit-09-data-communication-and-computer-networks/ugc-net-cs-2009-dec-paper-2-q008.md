# Question 8

*UGC NET CS · 2009 Dec Paper 2 · Cloud Computing and IoT · Cloud and Database Storage*

The highest noise margin is offered by

- **A.** BICMOS
- **B.** TTL
- **C.** ECL
- **D.** CMOS

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: D. CMOS**

## Solution

CMOS logic normally swings close to both supply rails, providing a large separation between guaranteed output levels and input thresholds. This produces a higher static noise margin than the listed bipolar logic families.

## Key Points

- Wide rail-to-rail logic swings generally provide generous noise margins.

## Why the other options are incorrect

TTL and ECL use smaller voltage-level separations; ECL prioritizes speed. BiCMOS combines technologies but the standard family comparison credits CMOS with the greatest noise immunity.
