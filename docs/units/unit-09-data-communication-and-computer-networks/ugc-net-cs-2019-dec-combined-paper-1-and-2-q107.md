# Question 107

*UGC NET CS · 2019 Dec Paper 1 And 2 · World Wide Web · DNS and ICANN*

The full form of ICANN is

- **1.** Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers
- **2.** Internet Corporation for Assigned Numbers and Names
- **3.** Institute of Cooperation for Assigned Names and Numbers
- **4.** Internet Connection for Assigned Names and Numbers

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 1. Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers**

## Solution

ICANN expands to Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers. Its name reflects its coordination role for the Internet's domain-name system and unique identifier systems. The exact expansion is option 1.

## Key Points

- ICANN = Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Option 2 reverses Names and Numbers. Option 3 changes both Internet Corporation and the organization's purpose, while option 4 substitutes Connection for Corporation. Acronym questions require every word and its order to match.
