# Question 115

*UGC NET CS · 2018 Dec Paper 1 And 2 · Wireless Networks · Mobile Generations and Wideband CDMA*

Third-generation mobile phones are digital and based on:

- **1.** AMPS
- **2.** D-AMPS
- **3.** CDMA
- **4.** Broadband CDMA

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 4. Broadband CDMA**

## Solution

Third-generation mobile systems such as UMTS use wideband code-division multiple access (W-CDMA), called ‘Broadband CDMA’ in the options. Its wider radio channel supports the higher data rates associated with 3G. Therefore option 4 is correct.

## Key Points

- Generation cue: 1G AMPS, 2G D-AMPS/cdmaOne, 3G W-CDMA (broadband CDMA).

## Why the other options are incorrect

AMPS is a first-generation analog cellular system. D-AMPS is a second-generation digital system. Narrowband cdmaOne/IS-95 is also generally classified as 2G; 3G moves to wideband/broadband CDMA families.
