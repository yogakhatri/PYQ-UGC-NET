# Question 47

*UGC NET CS · 2010 June Paper 2 · Cloud Computing and IoT · Cloud and Database Storage*

A leased special high-speed connection from the local telephone carrier for business users that transmits at 1.544 mbps is known as ________ carrier.

- **A.** T 1
- **B.** T 2
- **C.** T 3
- **D.** T 4

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: A. T 1**

## Solution

A T1 digital carrier provides a nominal 1.544 Mbit/s leased line in the North American hierarchy. It consists of 24 DS0 channels plus framing overhead.

## Key Points

- Remember the base high-speed leased digital carrier: T1 = 1.544 Mbit/s.

## Why the other options are incorrect

T2, T3, and T4 are successively higher-rate aggregate levels; T3, for example, is about 44.736 Mbit/s.
