# Question 48

*UGC NET CS · 2009 Dec Paper 2 · Mobile Technology · Fixed wireless broadband*

Which technology is sometime referred to as wireless cable ?

- **A.** MMDS
- **B.** ATM
- **C.** LMDS
- **D.** CDMA

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: A. MMDS**

## Solution

MMDS (Multichannel Multipoint Distribution Service) distributes multiple channels from a fixed transmitter to subscriber antennas using microwave radio. Because it can provide cable-like multichannel service without physical coaxial cable, it has historically been called 'wireless cable.'

## Key Points

- MMDS is a point-to-multipoint microwave distribution system historically marketed as wireless cable.

## Why the other options are incorrect

ATM is a cell-switching technology and CDMA a multiple-access technique. LMDS is also a fixed wireless access technology, but the historical nickname in this question is conventionally associated with MMDS.
