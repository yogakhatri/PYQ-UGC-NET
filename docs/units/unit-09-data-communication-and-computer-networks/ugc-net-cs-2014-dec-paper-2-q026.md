# Question 26

*UGC NET CS · 2014 Dec Paper 2 · Data Communication · Period-Frequency Conversion*

The period of a signal is 10 ms. What is its frequency in Hertz ?

- **A.** 10
- **B.** 100
- **C.** 1000
- **D.** 10000

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: B. 100**

## Solution

Frequency is the reciprocal of period. Convert 10 ms to seconds: 10 ms=10×10^−3 s=0.01 s. Therefore f=1/T=1/0.01=100 Hz.

## Key Points

- f=1/T with T in seconds.

## Why the other options are incorrect

10 ignores the millisecond-to-second conversion. 1000 and 10000 correspond to periods of 1 ms and 0.1 ms, respectively, not 10 ms.
