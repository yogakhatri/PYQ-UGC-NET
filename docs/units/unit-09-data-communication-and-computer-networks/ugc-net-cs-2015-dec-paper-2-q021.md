# Question 21

*UGC NET CS · 2015 Dec Paper 2 · Wireless LANs · IEEE 802.11 Distribution Services*

Which of the following services is not provided by wireless access point in 802.11 WLAN ?

- **1.** Association
- **2.** Disassociation
- **3.** Error correction
- **4.** Integration

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 3. Error correction**

## Solution

IEEE 802.11 distribution-system services include association, reassociation, disassociation, distribution, and integration. An access point participates in association/disassociation and integration between the wireless distribution system and another LAN. ‘Error correction’ is not one of the named 802.11 access-point services in this list.

## Key Points

- Distinguish named 802.11 distribution services from general communication functions.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Association registers a station with an AP, disassociation ends that relationship, and integration transfers frames between the 802.11 distribution system and a non-802.11 LAN. Those three are defined services.
