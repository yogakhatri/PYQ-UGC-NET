# Question 30

*UGC NET CS · 2010 June Paper 2 · Data Communication · Digital and Analog Transmission*

What is the maximum operating rate of a wireless LAN using infrared communication ?

- **A.** 1 mbps
- **B.** 2 mbps
- **C.** 5 mbps
- **D.** 11mbps

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: B. 2 mbps**

## Solution

The original IEEE 802.11 infrared physical layer specified data rates of 1 and 2 Mbit/s. Its maximum operating rate is therefore 2 Mbit/s.

## Key Points

- Legacy 802.11 infrared supported 1 or 2 Mbit/s.

## Why the other options are incorrect

1 Mbit/s is the lower supported rate; 5 and 11 Mbit/s are not rates of that original infrared PHY (11 Mbit/s is associated with 802.11b radio).
