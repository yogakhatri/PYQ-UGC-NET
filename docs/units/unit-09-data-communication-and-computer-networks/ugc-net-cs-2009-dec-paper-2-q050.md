# Question 50

*UGC NET CS · 2009 Dec Paper 2 · Cloud Computing and IoT · Cloud and Database Storage*

The unlicensed National Information Infrastructure band operates at the _________ frequency

- **A.** 2.4 GHz
- **B.** 5 GHz
- **C.** 33 MHz
- **D.** 5 MHz _________

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: B. 5 GHz**

## Solution

The Unlicensed National Information Infrastructure (U-NII) allocations are in the 5 GHz region (split into several sub-bands). Therefore the appropriate listed frequency is 5 GHz.

## Key Points

- U-NII is the unlicensed 5-GHz-band family used by technologies including 802.11a.

## Why the other options are incorrect

2.4 GHz is associated with the ISM band used by 802.11b/g and other devices; 33 MHz and 5 MHz are far outside the U-NII microwave allocations.
