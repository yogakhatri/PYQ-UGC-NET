# Question 42

*UGC NET CS · 2018 July Paper 2 · Data Communication · Signal Period and Frequency*

The period of a signal is 100 ms. Its frequency is _________.

- **1.** 10³ Hz
- **2.** 10⁻² kHz
- **3.** 10⁻³ kHz
- **4.** 10⁵ Hz

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 2. 10⁻² kHz**

## Solution

Frequency is the reciprocal of period. Here T=100 ms=0.1 s, so f=1/0.1=10 Hz. Since 1 kHz=1000 Hz, 10 Hz=0.01 kHz=10⁻² kHz. Therefore option 2 is correct.

## Key Points

- f=1/T; convert the period to seconds before taking the reciprocal.

## Why the other options are incorrect

10³ Hz and 10⁵ Hz are much too high for a 0.1-second period. 10⁻³ kHz equals only 1 Hz, corresponding to a 1-second period.
