# Question 47

*UGC NET CS · 2015 June Paper 3 · Software Design · Stepwise Refinement and Abstraction*

A Design concept Refinement is a :

- **1.** Top-down approach
- **2.** Complementary of Abstraction concept
- **3.** Process of elaboration
- **4.** All of the above

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 4. All of the above**

## Solution

Refinement starts with a high-level solution and repeatedly elaborates it into more detailed design and algorithmic steps, so it is top-down and is literally a process of elaboration. It complements abstraction: abstraction suppresses detail to expose essentials, while refinement reintroduces the necessary detail in controlled stages. All three descriptions are true.

## Key Points

- Abstraction hides detail; stepwise refinement progressively supplies it from the top down.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Options 1–3 are individually correct but incomplete because the question's design concept has all three properties. The all-inclusive choice is therefore required.
