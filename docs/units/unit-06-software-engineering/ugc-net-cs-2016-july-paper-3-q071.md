# Question 71

*UGC NET CS · 2016 July Paper 3 · Information Systems · MIS, DSS, and TPS Characteristics*

Which of the following is characteristic of an MIS ?

- **1.** Provides guidance in identifying problems, finding and evaluating alternative solutions, and selecting or comparing alternatives.
- **2.** Draws on diverse yet predictable data resources to aggregate and summarize data.
- **3.** High volume, data capture focus.
- **4.** Has as its goal the efficiency of data movement and processing and interfacing different TPS.

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 2. Draws on diverse yet predictable data resources to aggregate and summarize data.**

## Solution

A Management Information System converts routine organizational data into structured summaries and reports for managers. It draws on several known, recurring data sources and aggregates them into predictable information products. That is the description in option 2.

## Key Points

- TPS records operations; MIS summarizes recurring data for management; DSS supports less-structured choices among alternatives.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Option 1 describes a Decision Support System, which helps formulate problems and compare alternatives. Option 3 describes a Transaction Processing System's high-volume data-capture role. Option 4 emphasizes efficient movement, processing, and integration of transaction streams rather than the management-reporting role that distinguishes an MIS.
