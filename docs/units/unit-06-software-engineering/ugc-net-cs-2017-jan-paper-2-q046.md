# Question 46

*UGC NET CS · 2017 Jan Paper 2 · E-Business Models · Affiliate, Viral, Group-Purchasing, and Barter Models*

Match the following : List – I List – II a. Affiliate Marketing i. Vendors ask partners to place logos on partner’s site. If customers click, come to vendors and buy. b. Viral Marketing ii. Spread your brand on the net by word- of-mouth. Receivers will send your information to their friends. c. Group Purchasing iii. Aggregating the demands of small buyers to get a large volume. Then negotiate a price. d. Bartering Online iv. Exchanging surplus products and services with the process administered completely online by an intermediary. Company receives “points” for its contribution. Codes : a b c d

- **1.** i ii iii iv
- **2.** i iii ii iv
- **3.** iii ii iv i
- **4.** ii iii i iv

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 1. i ii iii iv**

## Solution

Affiliate marketing uses partner links or logos that send prospective buyers to a vendor, so a→i. Viral marketing spreads a message through recipients' word-of-mouth sharing, so b→ii. Group purchasing aggregates many small buyers to gain volume bargaining power, so c→iii. Online bartering exchanges surplus goods or services through an intermediary and may use credits or points, so d→iv. The code i,ii,iii,iv is option 1.

## Key Points

- Affiliate=referral; viral=sharing; group purchase=aggregate demand; barter=exchange without ordinary cash settlement.

## Why the other options are incorrect

The other codes interchange mechanisms with different sources of value: referrals, social propagation, pooled demand, and reciprocal exchange. Each description in List II directly names one mechanism.
