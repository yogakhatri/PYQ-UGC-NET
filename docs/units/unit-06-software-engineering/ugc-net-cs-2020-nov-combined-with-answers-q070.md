# Question 70

*UGC NET CS · 2020 Nov With Answers · Software Maintenance · Preventive Maintenance and Restructuring*

Modifying the software by restructuring is called

- **1.** Adaptive maintenance
- **2.** Corrective maintenance
- **3.** Perfective maintenance
- **4.** Preventive maintenance

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 4. Preventive maintenance**

## Solution

Restructuring changes internal organization to make software easier and safer to maintain without primarily correcting a reported fault or adapting to a new environment. It is preventive maintenance: improving code or data structure now reduces the likelihood and cost of future failures and changes. Therefore option 4.

## Key Points

- Preventive maintenance improves internal maintainability before a failure; restructuring is its classic technique.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Corrective maintenance fixes discovered defects. Adaptive maintenance responds to environmental changes such as a new platform. Perfective maintenance adds or improves user-visible capability or performance. Restructuring for future maintainability best matches prevention.
