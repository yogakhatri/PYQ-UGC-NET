# Question 103

*UGC NET CS · 2023 Mar 11 Shift 2 Dec 2022 Session · Software Process Models · Adaptive Software Development*

How do you create agile processes to manage unpredictability? A. Kequirements gathering must be conducted very caretully. B. Risk analysis must be conducted before planning takes place. C. Software increments must be delivered in short time periods. D. Software processes must not adapt to changes incrementally. Choose the correct answer from the options given below:

- **1.** A only
- **2.** B only
- **3.** Conly
- **4.** D only

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 3. Conly**

## Solution

Agile development manages uncertainty through short iterations that deliver working increments, obtain feedback, and permit priorities and design to adapt. Thus C is the defining practice.

## Key Points

- Short feedback cycles turn unpredictable change into manageable incremental decisions.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Exhaustive up-front requirements or risk analysis cannot eliminate unpredictability, while D directly contradicts agile adaptation.
