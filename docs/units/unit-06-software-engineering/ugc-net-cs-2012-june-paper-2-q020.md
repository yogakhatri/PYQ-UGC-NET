# Question 20

*UGC NET CS · 2012 June Paper 2 · Software Engineering · Cost and Schedule Objectives*

Main aim of software engineering is to produce

- **A.** program
- **B.** software
- **C.** within budget
- **D.** software within budget in the given schedule

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: D. software within budget in the given schedule**

## Solution

Software engineering aims to deliver software that satisfies required quality and functionality while respecting project constraints. Among the options, 'software within budget in the given schedule' is the only answer that includes the product plus the two central management constraints of cost and time.

## Key Points

- Successful software engineering balances scope/quality, cost and schedule.

## Why the other options are incorrect

A program may be only one component and says nothing about quality or constraints. 'Software' alone omits delivery feasibility. 'Within budget' alone does not name the product and omits schedule.
