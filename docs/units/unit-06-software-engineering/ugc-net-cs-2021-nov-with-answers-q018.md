# Question 18

*UGC NET CS · 2021 Nov With Answers · Software Process Improvement · Capability Maturity Model Levels*

Identify the correct order of the following five levels of Capability Maturity Model (from lower to higher) to measure the maturity of an organisation's software process. A. Defined B. Optimizing C. Initial D. Managed E. Repeatable Choose the correct answer from the options given below

- **1.** C, A, E, D, B
- **2.** C, E, A, B, D
- **3.** C, B, D, E, A
- **4.** C, E, A, D, B

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 4. C, E, A, D, B**

## Solution

The five original CMM maturity levels ascend as Initial, Repeatable, Defined, Managed, and Optimizing. Translating those names to the letters gives C, E, A, D, B, which is option 4.

## Key Points

- CMM ladder: Initial → Repeatable → Defined → Managed → Optimizing.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Options 1–3 move Defined, Repeatable, Managed, or Optimizing out of their maturity sequence. The progression is from ad hoc work to repeatable projects, organization-wide definition, quantitative management, and continuous optimization.
