# Question 45

*UGC NET CS · 2015 June Paper 2 · Software Project Management · Project Planning Activities*

Which one from the following is highly associated activity of project planning ?

- **1.** Keep track of the project progress.
- **2.** Compare actual and planned progress and costs.
- **3.** Identify the activities, milestones and deliverables produced by a project.
- **4.** Both (2) and (3).

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 3. Identify the activities, milestones and deliverables produced by a project.**

## Solution

Project planning defines the work before execution: it identifies activities, milestones, deliverables, dependencies, resources, and the schedule. Thus identifying activities, milestones, and deliverables is the planning activity in option 3.

## Key Points

- Planning establishes activities, milestones, deliverables, estimates, and schedules; monitoring compares actual progress with that plan.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Keeping track of progress and comparing actual results or costs with the plan are monitoring and control activities performed after work begins. Option 4 mixes one control activity with one planning activity, so it is not the clean planning-only answer.
