# Question 48

*UGC NET CS · 2015 Dec Paper 3 · Information Systems · Computer-System Acquisition: Purchase, Lease and Rental*

As compared to rental and leasing methods to acquire computer systems for a Management Information System (MIS), purchase method has following advantage :

- **1.** It has high level of flexibility
- **2.** It doesn't require cash up-front
- **3.** It is a business investment
- **4.** Little risk of obsolescence

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 3. It is a business investment**

## Solution

Buying the computer system converts the expenditure into ownership of a business asset, so it is a business investment. The organization obtains control of the equipment and may capitalize it and use it throughout its economic life.

## Key Points

- Purchase brings ownership and asset value; leasing and rental mainly bring flexibility and reduced obsolescence exposure.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Purchase normally requires substantial up-front cash, gives less short-term flexibility than renting, and leaves the buyer exposed to technological obsolescence. Those three features are disadvantages of purchase or advantages of rental/leasing, not purchase advantages.
