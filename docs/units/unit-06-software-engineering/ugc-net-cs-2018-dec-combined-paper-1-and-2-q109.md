# Question 109

*UGC NET CS · 2018 Dec Paper 1 And 2 · Software Development Methods and Tools · Agile Principles*

Which is not one of the principles of an agile software development method?

- **1.** Customer involvement
- **2.** Embrace change
- **3.** Incremental delivery
- **4.** Following the plan

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 4. Following the plan**

## Solution

Agile development emphasizes close customer collaboration, welcoming changing requirements, and delivering useful software incrementally. It values responding to change over following a fixed plan. Consequently ‘following the plan’ is the item that is not an agile principle, option 4.

## Key Points

- Agile plans are adaptable: customer feedback, change, and frequent increments take priority over rigid adherence to an initial plan.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Customer involvement gives rapid feedback, embracing change allows the product to adapt, and incremental delivery supplies working value in small releases; all three are central agile ideas. A plan can guide work, but strict plan conformance is not valued above learning and change.
