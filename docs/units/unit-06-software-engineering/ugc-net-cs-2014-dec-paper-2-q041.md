# Question 41

*UGC NET CS · 2014 Dec Paper 2 · Software Process Models · Umbrella Activities*

_____ are applied throughout the software process.

- **A.** Framework activities
- **B.** Umbrella activities
- **C.** Planning activities
- **D.** Construction activities

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: B. Umbrella activities**

## Solution

Umbrella activities span the entire software process and support every framework activity. Examples include project tracking and control, risk management, software quality assurance, technical reviews, measurement, configuration management, reuse management and work-product preparation.

## Key Points

- Umbrella activities are cross-cutting controls and support practices that continue across all process stages.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Framework activities such as communication, planning, modeling, construction and deployment form the process skeleton but occur as identifiable workflow stages. Planning and construction are individual framework activities, not cross-cutting activities applied throughout.
