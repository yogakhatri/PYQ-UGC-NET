# Question 43

*UGC NET CS · 2015 June Paper 2 · Software Process Models · Waterfall or Classic Life Cycle*

Which process model is also called as classic life cycle model ?

- **1.** Waterfall model
- **2.** RAD model
- **3.** Prototyping model
- **4.** Incremental model

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 1. Waterfall model**

## Solution

The waterfall model is traditionally called the classic life-cycle or linear-sequential model. It arranges major activities—requirements, design, implementation, testing, deployment, and maintenance—in a largely sequential flow. Therefore option 1 is correct.

## Key Points

- Classic life cycle = waterfall = linear sequential development.

## Why the other options are incorrect

RAD emphasizes rapid, time-boxed construction; prototyping builds an early model to refine understanding; and incremental development delivers the system in successive usable portions. None of these is the model historically labeled the classic life cycle.
