# Question 42

*UGC NET CS · 2017 Jan Paper 2 · Software Quality · McCall and ISO 9126 Factors*

Which of the following is not one of three software product aspects addressed by McCall’s software quality factors ?

- **1.** Ability to undergo change
- **2.** Adaptiability to new environments
- **3.** Operational characteristics
- **4.** Production costs and scheduling

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 4. Production costs and scheduling**

## Solution

McCall groups software quality factors into product operation, product revision, and product transition. Operational characteristics correspond to product operation; ability to undergo change corresponds to revision; and adaptability to new environments corresponds to transition. Production costs and scheduling are project-management concerns, not one of these three product-quality aspects. Therefore option 4 is correct.

## Key Points

- McCall's three views: operate the product, revise the product, transition the product.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Options 1, 2, and 3 are plain-language descriptions of revision, transition, and operation respectively, so each belongs to McCall's framework.
