# Question 109

*UGC NET CS · 2019 June Paper 1 And 2 · Software Quality · Risk monitoring*

Which are primary objectives of risk monitoring in software project tracking? P: Assess whether predicted risks actually occur. Q: Ensure that defined risk-aversion steps are properly applied. R: Collect information usable for future risk analysis.

- **1.** Only P and Q
- **2.** Only P and R
- **3.** Only Q and R
- **4.** All of P, Q and R

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 4. All of P, Q and R**

## Solution

Risk monitoring checks whether predicted risks actually materialize, whether planned mitigation or avoidance actions are being applied and effective, and what evidence should be retained for future risk identification and estimation. P, Q and R are therefore all primary monitoring objectives.

## Key Points

- Risk monitoring tracks risk occurrence, response execution and reusable evidence for future projects.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Options 1–3 each omit one legitimate part of monitoring: occurrence tracking, response-plan compliance or organizational learning.
