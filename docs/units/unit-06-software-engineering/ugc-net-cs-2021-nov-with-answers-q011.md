# Question 11

*UGC NET CS · 2021 Nov With Answers · Software Testing · Alpha and Beta Testing*

In software testing, beta testing is the testing performed by _______________.

- **1.** potential customers at the developer's location
- **2.** potential customers at their own locations
- **3.** product developers at the customer's location
- **4.** product developers at their own locations

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 2. potential customers at their own locations**

## Solution

Beta testing places a prerelease product with representative external users in their normal environments. Those potential customers exercise the software at their own locations and report real-world failures and usability problems. This is option 2.

## Key Points

- Alpha: selected users at the developer site; beta: external users in their own environments.

## Why the other options are incorrect

Testing by users at the developer's site is alpha testing. Testing by product developers, whether at their own or a customer's site, is not the defining beta arrangement.
