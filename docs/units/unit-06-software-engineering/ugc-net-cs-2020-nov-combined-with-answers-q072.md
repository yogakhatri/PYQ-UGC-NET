# Question 72

*UGC NET CS · 2020 Nov With Answers · Estimation and Scheduling · Function-Point Productivity and Cost*

A project is estimated at 864 function points. Its six-person team has one requirements analyst (₹50,000/month), one designer (₹70,000/month), two programmers (₹60,000/month each), and two testers (₹60,000/month each). Average productivity is 12 FP per person-month. What is the projected staffing cost?

- **1.** ₹33,20,000
- **2.** ₹43,20,000
- **3.** ₹33,10,000
- **4.** ₹22,10,000

> [!TIP]
> **Correct answer: 2. ₹43,20,000**

## Solution

At 12 function points per person-month, 864 FP requires 864/12=72 person-months. With six people working together, duration is 72/6=12 months. Monthly team salary is ₹50,000+₹70,000+2×₹60,000+2×₹60,000=₹3,60,000. Multiplying by 12 months gives ₹43,20,000. Therefore option 2.

## Key Points

- Effort=FP/productivity; duration=effort/team size; cost=duration×sum of monthly salaries.

## Why the other options are incorrect

The other totals omit a role, use the productivity as team-month productivity rather than person-month productivity, or multiply by the wrong duration. The salary mix matters even though all six contribute to the person-month estimate.
