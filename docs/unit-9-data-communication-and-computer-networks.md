## Unit-wide exam playbook

- **Core idea:** Locate the question at the correct layer, draw the frame/packet path, and label addresses, headers, windows, or signal parameters.
- **Method:** Normalize bit/byte and second units. For subnetting split prefix and host bits; for error control perform modulo-2 arithmetic; for sliding windows trace sequence numbers.
- **Rules/formulas:** Transmission delay = length/rate; propagation delay = distance/speed. Hosts per IPv4 subnet are normally 2^h-2. Nyquist and Shannon answer different channel assumptions.
- **Frequent traps:** Do not mix MAC, IP and port addresses; bandwidth with throughput; forwarding with routing; CRC polynomial division with ordinary subtraction.
